# Quick Start Guide

Get your music and events platform up and running in minutes. No coding required ‚Äî everything is done through the admin dashboard.

---

## Step 1: Open Your Site

Once the app is running, you'll see the landing page with placeholder content. Everything you see ‚Äî text, images, fonts, colours ‚Äî can be customised from the admin dashboard.

---

## Step 2: Open the Admin Dashboard

1. Tap the **hamburger menu** (three lines) in the top-right corner
2. Select **Admin**
3. You'll see the dashboard with 13 sections along the top

---

## Step 3: Set Up Your Branding

### Global Branding (Section 1)
- Enter your **company or event name** ‚Äî this appears in the top ribbon on every page
- Upload your **logo** ‚Äî it replaces the placeholder in the header bar

### Style Guide (Section 2)
- Set your **primary colour**, heading colour, and background colour
- Choose **heading and body fonts** from the dropdown
- Upload a **custom font** (.ttf, .otf, .woff, .woff2) if you have one ‚Äî it will automatically become available in the font dropdowns

---

## Step 4: Add Your Content

### Landing Page (Section 7)
- Set the **heading text** that appears at the top of the home page
- Customise the **search placeholder** text
- Set the **enquiry form title**
- Upload a **banner image** for the bottom of the page

### Wallpapers (Section 3)
- Upload **background images** for any page: Home, Artists, Events, DS, and Login
- Each wallpaper gets a semi-transparent overlay so your text stays readable

### Social Media (Section 4)
- Paste your links for **Instagram, Facebook, X, TikTok, YouTube, SoundCloud, Spotify, Bandcamp, your website, and email**
- Only platforms with URLs filled in will show up on the landing page

### Animations (Section 5)
- Set up to **3 animation boxes** that appear on the landing page
- For each box, enter **text**, choose an **animation style** (fade-in, slide-up, slide-left, slide-right, zoom-in, bounce, or pulse), and optionally upload a **background image**

---

## Step 5: Add Artists

### Manage Artists (Section 8)
1. Click **Add Artist**
2. Fill in the name and any other details ‚Äî genre, description, bio, origin, members, links, images
3. Use the **eye icons** next to each field to show/hide that field on the public page
4. Click **Save** when done
5. To feature an artist on the home page grid, check the **Featured** toggle

### Bulk Import
- Click the **CSV Import** card to upload a spreadsheet of artists
- Download a **CSV Export** first to see the expected column format

---

## Step 6: Add Events

### Manage Events (Section 9)
1. Click **Add Event**
2. Fill in the name, date, time, venue, address, description, and ticket URL
3. Add a **Google Maps URL** to embed a map on the event detail page
4. Use **eye icons** to control which fields are visible
5. Click **Save**

---

## Step 7: Set Up DS Client Profiles

### DS Page (Section 12)
- Set the **page title** and **content text** for the DS page
- Upload a **content image**
- Add **client profiles** with the same rich fields as artists (images, bio, contact, links, members, visibility toggles)

---

## Step 8: Add Media

Go to **Integrations** (from the hamburger menu) and scroll to the **Media Manager** at the bottom.

1. Enter a **title** and **URL** for your media
2. Choose the **type**: YouTube, Bandcamp, SoundCloud, or Audio
3. Click **Add**
4. Media items appear in the player on the landing page

**Supported media:**
- YouTube video URLs
- Bandcamp embed URLs
- SoundCloud track URLs
- Direct audio file URLs

---

## Step 9: Configure Navigation

### Navigation (Section 13)
- Rename the **bottom nav buttons** (Home, Artists, Events, DS, Profile)
- Toggle which items appear in the **hamburger menu** (including Donate, Admin, and Integrations)

---

## Step 10: Set Up the Login Page

### Login Page (Section 6)
- Set the **welcome text** and **subtitle**
- Upload a **header image** for the login page

---

## Step 11: Enable Integrations (Optional)

Go to **Integrations** from the hamburger menu.

### Google Sheets
- Toggle Google Sheets on
- Enter your **Sheet ID** and **sheet name** for enquiries and/or donations
- Form submissions will automatically be logged to your spreadsheet

### AI Assistant
- Toggle on and enter your **API key** (OpenAI or Anthropic)
- Choose your **model** and **provider**

### Donations
- Toggle donations on
- Set the **title**, **description**, and **suggested amounts**
- The Donate page will become available from the hamburger menu

### Other Integrations
- Toggle cards for Google Drive, Docs, Gmail, YouTube, YouTube Music, Bandcamp, DistroKid, and Stripe (Stripe is dormant ‚Äî ready for future activation)

---

## Step 12: Set Up Donations (Optional)

1. Go to **Integrations** and enable **Donations**
2. Set a **title**, **description**, and comma-separated **amounts** (e.g. "5,10,25,50,100")
3. The **/donate** page will display a form where visitors can choose an amount and submit
4. Optionally connect a **Google Sheet** to log donations automatically

---

## Tips

- **Changes save per section** ‚Äî click Save within each admin section, and changes appear immediately on the public pages
- **Visibility toggles** (eye icons) let you show/hide individual fields on artist, event, and DS client profiles without deleting the data
- **Everything is a placeholder** by default ‚Äî dashed boxes show where images will go, and bracket text like `[ Company Name ]` shows where your content will appear
- **Mobile-first** ‚Äî the layout is designed for phones but works on desktop too
- **Share buttons** appear on the landing page, artist detail, and event detail pages

---

## Need Help?

The admin dashboard is designed to be self-explanatory. Each section focuses on one part of the site. Work through them in order (1 through 13) for the best setup experience.
